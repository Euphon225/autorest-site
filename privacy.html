AutoRest – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

1. Data Collection

AutoRest does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All workout data (sets, heart rate, rest durations) stays exclusively on your device.

2. HealthKit

AutoRest uses Apple HealthKit to read heart rate data and save workout sessions. This data is stored locally on your device via Apple Health and is never shared with third parties.

3. In-App Purchases

AutoRest offers a one-time purchase ("AutoRest Pro") processed entirely by Apple. We do not store any payment information.

4. Third-Party Services

AutoRest does not use any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking services.

5. Children's Privacy

AutoRest does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
